’od sruṅ ’di lta ste dper na phyogs daṅ phyogs mtshams tha dad pa nas kluṅ chen po’i chu’i phuṅ
po rgya mtsho chen por bab pa ni ’di lta ste | lan tshwa’i ror ro gcig par ’gyur ro ||
’od sruṅ de bźin du byaṅ chub sems dpa’i dge ba’i rtsa ba sgo tha dad pa nas bsags pa byaṅ
chub tu yoṅs su bsṅos pa thams cad kyaṅ ’di lta ste | thams cad mkhyen pa ñid kyi ror ro
gcig par ’gyur ro || de la ’di skad ces bya ste| dper na tha dad phyogs kyi chu kluṅ rnams
|| rgya mtsho cher bab ro gcig ’gyur ba bźin || tha dad sgo nas dge ba bsags byas pa ||
byaṅ chub bsṅos pa rnams kyaṅ ro gcig ’gyur ||
